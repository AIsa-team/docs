AIsa is the unified resource and payment layer for the AI economy. Route requests to 50+ state-of-the-art LLMs (OpenAI, Anthropic, Gemini, Qwen, DeepSeek, and more), access real-time web, financial, and social data from 100+ APIs, and let autonomous agents pay for their own compute — all through one API key.

## How it works

<Steps>
  <Step title="One API key, every model">
    Swap your `base_url` to AIsa and instantly access GPT, Claude, Gemini, and 50+ other LLMs. All endpoints are OpenAI-compatible, so your existing SDKs just work.
  </Step>
  <Step title="One gateway, every data source">
    Access 100+ specialized APIs — Twitter, Perplexity, Tavily, YouTube, stock prices, prediction markets — without managing separate vendor accounts.
  </Step>
  <Step title="One wallet, autonomous payments">
    Fund a single AIsa wallet with fiat or USDC. Every endpoint supports the x402 machine payment protocol, so agents can pay for compute and data on their own.
  </Step>
</Steps>
